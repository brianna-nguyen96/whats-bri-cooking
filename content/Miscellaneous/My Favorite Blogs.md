I love blogs! Something about them feels so retro and chic, the permanence of a blog post vs. newsletter is so endearing to me. A blog is a living thing, and I love returning to my favorites after a long time away. This page is more to jot down what I like, lots of messy notes:

getting lost in the internet again, blogrolls
- https://ooh.directory
- https://blogroll.org

food blogs:
- [mmm-yoso](https://mmm-yoso.com)
	- the GOAT of San Diego food bloggers as far as I'm concerned
- [Annie & Alison](https://www.annievarberg.com/annie-and-alison-food-blog)
	- cooking the entirety of Dining In and Nothing Fancy. Great to refer to when I'm making one of Alison's recipes & lovely writing as well!
- [goodstuffnw](https://goodstuffnw.com)
	- highlighting local produce, good food inspo
- [Do You Know the Muffin Man?](https://www.gnufmuffin.com)
	- desserts
- [Fro-Yo Girl](https://froyogirl.blogspot.com)
	- man, I really get a kick out of this fro yo blog. heavy regional emphasis on Southern CA and local specials/promotions. quite prodigiously updated, I must say
- [Ultimate Mai Tai](https://ultimatemaitai.com)
	- tiki drink history & reviews

random blogs:
